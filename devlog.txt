LESSONS LEARNED
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
- Remember that other people are going to be using your code/ seeing what your displaying
- GitHub conflicts (bane of my existence)/ try not to delete what your partners did
- Comments really important especially if the team isn't use to a new tool(Processing)
- Learning Together, much easier than learning by yourself
- Bugtesting is important!
- Somethings look better than others/ layout can be very helpful
- But don't waste time on beautifying our project
- Don't forget about the devlog and wait till the end to finish it. (lol)
- For processing, the displays are instantaneous, much like SOP statements;
use that to advantage.
